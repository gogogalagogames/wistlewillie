MORE ABOUT THIS GAME

Plot: In the world of Zelanax, all living things has acquired immortality. This has caused the world to stagnate, and allowed for horrifying tyrants to dominate the world. Our hero, given the ability to trap the essence of living beings, becomes tasked with putting an end to eternity by killing its source, Corr.

COMBAT:

  • I-frame dashes.
  • Stamina/energy management.
  • Visceral melee & gunplay combat.



BOSS FIGHTS:

  • Be prepared to face CORR's vast roster of challenging multi-phase boss encounters.
  • Non-linear progression. Stuck on a boss fight? Try another for a while.
  • Tired of not being able to replay a boss fight you enjoyed? In CORR, all boss fights can be replayed through memory shards.



CHARACTER PROGRESSION:

  • Acquire talismans to customize your character build with passive abilities.
  • Acquire unique berserk powers that can be activated during combat.
  • Collect ingredients and brew your own consumables.
  • Obtain Hexes that increase the game's difficulty in unique ways in order to challenge yourself and obtain unique rewards.



UNCOVER THE TRUTH:

  • Piece together the events of the stagnant world by interpreting item descriptions, cryptic character dialogue, environmental storytelling, and more.
  • Includes branching NPC questlines.

THE TEAM:


About the team:


Go Go Galago Games is a two man Game Developer based in Grenaa, Denmark.

As part of their computer science studies, Dani and Mikkel were given the awesome opportunity of interning in their own Company, which lead to them founding Go Go Galago Games. Driven by their passion for all things game development, production on CORR began mid August 2022. The goal with CORR was to provide the world with the best Boss Rush game the world has seen, which continues to be their primary objective. Apart from a three month internship by Ruuben Sonn, CORR continues to be developed by a two man dev team.

Why did you choose a Galago as mascot?
No matter how hard we try, we always end up sleeping during the day and working throughout the night. All Galago species are nocturnal, so we found it rather fitting. Also, the Galago is a pretty cool animal. Fight us!
