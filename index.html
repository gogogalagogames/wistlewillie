MORE ABOUT THIS GAME

Whistle Willie is an open-world survival horror game by three indie studios at Spelkollektivet, Sweden’s unique game developer hotel. Inspired by Choo-Choo Charles, Whistle Willie shifts mobile base exploration from railways to the vast open sea.

Feature List:

  • Explore open-world islands filled with hidden secrets and deadly threats.
  • Defend yourself against Willie, the relentless steamboat spider stalking land and sea.
  • Upgrade your steamboat with weapons and fishing gear to survive.
  • Manage scarce resources - scavenge, fish, and upgrade to stay alive.
  • Collaborate with survivors, forging vital bonds.
  • Developed collaboratively by three indie teams at Spelkollektivet, Sweden’s unique game developer hotel.

About the studios:


KasperDev, CANCAT Games, and Go Go Galago Games are small indie studios who are currently working on their debut games, all being published through Spelkollektivet. After collaborating during a Spelkollektivet game jam, they joined forces to work on Whistle Willie as a shared passion project in their spare time, and to turn it into a full Steam release.
